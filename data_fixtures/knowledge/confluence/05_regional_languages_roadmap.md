# TOI app — regional language editions roadmap

## Currently shipped

- **English** — primary edition, all platforms
- **Hindi (Devanagari)** — full edition, all platforms

## In active development (target Q3 2026)

- **Marathi** — pilot edition rolling out to a 10% audience starting
  August 2026. Tracked as **TOI-4560**.
- **Bengali** — pilot following Marathi by ~6 weeks.

## Backlog (no committed date)

- Tamil
- Telugu
- Gujarati

## How to respond to user requests

When a user asks for a regional language edition, point them at this page
and confirm whether their requested language is in development, backlogged,
or out of scope. NRI users (London, Dubai, Singapore, Bay Area) make up
about 18% of regional-language requests; mention that all editions ship
worldwide via the app stores.
