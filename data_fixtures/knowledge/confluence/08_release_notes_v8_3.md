# TOI app — release notes v8.3.x

## v8.3.2 — 24 April 2026

Bug fixes:

- **TOI-4530** Paywall persists after successful TOI Plus payment (fixed)
- **TOI-4548** Sign-in stuck after OTP entry (fixed)
- Android 10/11 video player rollback (no JIRA — emergency revert)

## v8.3.1 — 18 April 2026

Bug fixes:

- Battery drain on Pixel 7 / Pixel 8 (background sync optimisations)
- Crash on opening Markets section without network (regression from 8.3.0)

Known issues introduced (will be fixed in v8.4):

- iOS 17.4+ video player crash (TOI-4521)
- iOS 17.4+ audio briefing playback hang (TOI-4550)

## v8.3.0 — 10 April 2026

New features:

- **Audio briefings** — twice-daily 5-minute audio summary
- Personalised section ordering
- TOI Plus Annual at ₹1499 (previously ₹1799)
